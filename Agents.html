VALORANT AGENTS



  DUELIST  

Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high frags and seek out engagements first.

PHOENIX

Phoenix, like his namesake, is all about fire, and rising from the ashes. With the ability to create walls of fire, throw
fireballs, and resurrect at a designated location with full health upon death, Phoenix is a force to be reckoned with.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Heating up Blaze (C) Curveball (Q) Hot Hands (E) Run It Back (X)



RAZE

Raze is another appropriately named Agent, as pretty much everything in her wake is rubble. Using Boom
Bots, Blast Packs, Cluster Grenades, and a rocket launcher, Raze will destroy anything and everything in her path.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Boom Bot (C) Blast Pack (Q) Paint Shells (E) Showstopper (X)



JETT

Jett uses her mastery of the air and winds to glide around the battlefield and get the drop on her opponents from
above.Her daggers can sail through the air at incredible speeds, and they rarely miss their target.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Drift Cloudburst (C) Updraft (Q) Tailwind (E) Blade Storm (X)



YORU

Yoru has the ability to create rifts in reality, and create mirror images of himself, or even become entirely invisible
to the enemy for a brief time. This level of espionage makes him a potent weapon in any scenario.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Fakeout (C) Blindside (Q) Gatecrash (E) Dimensional Rift (X)



NEON

Neon uses the power of electricity to blind opponents with walls of light, and enhances her own power and speed
with deadly lightning beams. Her spark can turn the tide of a fight, regardless of the odds.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Energy Fast Lane (C) Relay Bolt (Q) High Gear (E) Overdrive (X)



REYNA

Reyna has access to dark ethereal abilities that create Soul Orbs upon downing an enemy. She can consume
them to heal and become briefly invulnerable. Using Empress, her firing, equip and reload speeds all receive
a dramatic boost, making her a powerhouse in battle.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Soul Harvest Leer (C) Dismiss (E) Devour (Q) Empress (X)

  SENTINEL  

Sentinels are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attacker and defender rounds.



SAGE

A potent medic on the battlefield, Sage can fire Healing Orbs at damaged allies, whilst slowing enemies with
barriers and AOE slowing fields. Her Ultimate enables a full res for one of her allies, making her an invaluable
asset to any team.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Barrier Orb (C) Slow Orb (Q) Healing Orb (E) Resurrection (X)


CYPHER

A master of espionage, Cypher can place trapwires and cages across the map to ensnare unsuspecting
opponents. His darts and spycams let him survey the battlefield better than most others, and can even scan
for living enemies through the corpses of those he's slain.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Trapwire (C) Cyber Cage (Q) Spycam (E) Neural Theft (X)


CHAMBER

Chamber is an excellent all-rounder. With the ability to place scanning traps, summon a heavy pistol, create a
teleportation anchor, and the deadliest sniper rifle in the game, Chamber is an Agent you can rely on to keep
the enemy on their toes.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Trademark (C) Headhunter (Q) Rendevous (E) Tour De Force (X)


KILLJOY

KIlljoy lives up to her name when it comes to how the enemies will feel. Her Nanoswarm and Alarmbots can
distract and debuff enemies unfortunately enough to encounter them, whilst her turret and lockdown device
enables her to effectively close off certain lanes.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Nanoswarm (C) Alarmbot (Q) Turret (E) Lockdown (X)


DEADLOCK

Norwegian operative Deadlock deploys an array of cutting-edge nanowire to secure the battlefield from even
the most lethal assault. No one escapes her vigilant watch, nor survives

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Gravnet (C) Sonic Sensor (Q) Barrier Mesh(E) Annihilation (X)


  CONTROLLER  

Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.



OMEN

Omen has control over various orbs that completely block an opponent's vision, giving Omen the immediate
upper hand in a firefight. He can also enter an ethereal form to teleport around the map.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Shrouded Step (C) Paranoia (Q) Dark Cover (E) From The Shadows (X)


VIPER

Viper is much like the animal she takes her name from, using toxins and poison to disorient and eliminate her
foes. With huge AOE/CC potential, Viper is a formidable opponent to those trying to seize an area of the map
from her.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Fuel/Toxin Snake Bite (C) Poison Cloud (Q) Toxic Screen (E) Viper's Pit (X)


BRIMSTONE

Using payload technology, brimstone brings the fire by using his grenade launcher, smoke clouds, and orbital
strike laser to bombard the enemy with relentless and deadly armaments.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Fuel/Toxin Stim Beacon (C) Incendiary (Q) Sky Smoke (E) Orbital Strike (X)




ASTRA

Astra has the power to call on the cosmic forces of the universe, and activate stars on the battlefield that have
a number of different effects to choose from. Versatile, and more importantly, deadly, astrology and the
zodiacs have never seemed more terrifying.

1st Skill 2st Skill 3nd Skill 4rd Skill Ultimate
Gravity Well (C) Nova Pulse (Q) Nebula/Dissipate (E) Astral Form (X) Cosmic Divide (X)


HARBOR

An Agent with complete control of the ocean, Harbor can use the waves to create defensive spheres or walls
of water to protect or block who he wishes. He can also summon geysers of high powered water from the
ground that can easily disorient, or even wash away opponents completely.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Cascade (C) Cove (Q) High Tide (E) Reckoning (X)




  INITIATOR  

Initiators challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.



SOVA

A technologically advanced hunter, Sova uses his owl drone to tag enemies across the map, and he uses his
bow to fire various bolts that can apply various effects, from shock and recon, to wall-piercing energy blasts.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Owl Drone (C) Shock Bolt (Q) Recon Bolt (E) Hunter's Fury (X)




BREACH

Breach uses seismic energy to disorient his opponents with blinding charges, and can create quakes that
resonate across the entire map and dazes all who are caught in its waves.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Aftershock (C) Flashpoint (Q) Fault Line (E) Rolling Thunder (X)




SKYE

Skye uses her various trinkets to trigger various effects, summoning Seekers, a hawk, and a Tasmanian tiger
to deal drastic damage to a targeted enemy. But she can also use healing trinkets to heal those around her,
with the exception of herself.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Regrowth (C) Trailblazer (Q) Guiding Light (E) Seekers (X)




KAY/O

A robotic powerhouse, KAY/O can throw powerful flash and explosive grenades, as well as a suppression
blade with a delayed explosive timer. His Ultimate enables him to overload with energy, and emit pulses in a
radius that surpresses enemies caught in its waves.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
None Frag/Ment (C) Flash/Drive (Q) Zero/Point (E) Null/CMD (X)




FADE

Fade uses terror to her advantage, marking enemies with a dark mist that tracks them. Her Prowler can
nearsight enemies that it touches, her knot can hold enemies in place and deafen them, and she can emit a
wave of nightmare energy that decays any foe that it touches.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Terror Trail Prowler (C) Seize (Q) Haunt (E) Nightfall (X)




GEKKO

Gekko the Angeleno leads a tight-knit crew of calamitous creatures. His buddies bound forward, scattering
enemies out of the way, with Gekko chasing them down to regroup and go again.

Passive 1st Skill 2nd Skill 3rd Skill Ultimate
Globules Mosh Pit(C) Wingman(Q) Dizzy (E) Thrash (X)
